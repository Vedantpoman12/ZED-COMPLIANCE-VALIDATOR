Welcome to ZED Compliance Validator

This document verification system uses advanced machine learning models to detect, identify, and validate documents such as PAN cards, GST certificates, and Aadhaar cards.

What is AI?

Artificial intelligence (AI) is the intelligence of machines or software, as opposed to the intelligence of living beings, primarily of humans. It is a field of study in computer science that develops and studies intelligent machines. Such machines may be called AIs.

How it Works?

Upload Document:

Users upload a scanned image or PDF of the document.

AI Processing:

The ML model extracts key features such as logos, text patterns, and formats using OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and image analysis.

Classification:

The model identifies which type of document it is — PAN, Aadhaar, GST certificate, etc.

Verification:

The system checks for validity markers, format rules, and consistency to verify the document.

Result:

The verified result and document type are displayed instantly on the screen.
