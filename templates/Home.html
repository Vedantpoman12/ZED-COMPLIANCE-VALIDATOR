ZED Compliance Validator

ZED is a comprehensive document verification system designed to automate and simplify the validation of essential identity and compliance documents.

Key Features

1. Intelligent Document Recognition

Our system automatically identifies various document types, including PAN Cards, Aadhaar Cards, and GST Certificates, eliminating the need for manual sorting.

2. Advanced OCR Technology

Powered by Tesseract and Poppler, ZED extracts text from both images and PDF files with high precision, ensuring that critical data is captured accurately.

3. Automated Verification Logic

The system performs real-time validation checks, such as verifying PAN number formats (e.g., ABCDE1234F) and cross-referencing document patterns to detect potential discrepancies.

4. AI-Powered Assistant

Integrated with a smart chatbot trained on the Bronze Certification User Manual, ZED provides instant answers to your compliance queries and guides you through the verification process.

5. Seamless User Experience

With a clean, responsive interface, users can easily upload documents, view extraction results, and receive instant verification status reports.
